About Us

OPUS Digital Dental Solutions is committed to cultivating deep, enriching relationship with our clients and the dental community through leading the digital dental market in customized workflow solutions developed to specifically cater to each individual client. A tech-company at heart, we strive to bring our clients the best the digital dental world has to offer; in both products and service.

Honesty

Always the best policy! We are serious about being serious and teach our employees to continually do what is right, even if the consequences are not in our best interest. Not only that, we also hold ourselves to a very high standard of business edicate, encouraging our employees contact and/or act first when a problem arises.

Quality

We are only as good as the products/services we provide. That's why we here at OPUS are obsessed with bringing our clients the best of the digital dental world exclusively address every aspect of our client's dream niche.

Innovation

Technology is one of our passions and we devote a good amount of our time to ensure the products we carry are at the cusp of innovation. However, a true understanding of these products and how they can fit into existing or potential workflows is essential to making our client solutions simple and seemless; an aspect of our own innovation we are constantly striving to perfect.

Teamwork

As the saying goes, it makes the dream work; our dream of assiting you reach your true potential that is! Our strong belief in company unity extends beyond the scope of our offices and into your business. At the end of the day, you are part of our team too!

Feedback

Though natraully entwined with Teamwork, we felt that communication is so inherently important to us that it deserves it's own section! Feedback loops are an important part of any well-designed system as a way of checking and correcting potential emerging problems. In response we have built our company around the principle of listening to and accomodating clients and our own employees as a way of making adjustments to embody our belief in progrress.
